Closed Loop

2017, 2-channel digital video, 200 minute loop

Two artificial intelligence models have an active conversation reacting to each other in a constant feedback loop - one communicating with images, the other with words.

Technical Description

Two artificial intelligence models: a language captioning recurrent neural network writing what it sees in the images generated, and a generative neural network generating images responding to the words generated.
Two datasets to train the A.I. models: a dataset of 4.1 million captioned images to train a language network, and the Imagenet dataset of 14.2 million photographs to train the image generator network.

Single channel rendering of two channel video installation

Installation shot of Closed Loop shown as two back projected suspended screens, Slade Degree Show 2017
