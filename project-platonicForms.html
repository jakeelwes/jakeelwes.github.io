Platonic Forms (in progress)

Video & AI Code - 2016

The applied platonic form & essence of - fields and skies, computer hardware (self-image), toilets, spiritual places & temples & art.

I see this as the metaphysical problem of universals being visually realised using a digital model.

A convolutional neural network is trained to understand meaning and classify different objects after being fed many images, this can then be used to generate new instances & discrete forms from scratch. But what if it is asked to generate in an abstract sense, the physics defying & therefore impossible to visualise, universal form of a thing?

Materials

Public domain image dataset.
