taxonomy of sound

3D Prints - Chicago 2016

These specimens/fossils are 3D printed forms generated by improvising phrases on the piano in every consecutive key (in the manner of Bach’s Well-tempered Clavier). Custom software interprets the harmonics and frequencies being played and spins them into 3D forms, leaving a physical trace of the immaterial musical action.

7 out of a series of 24:
’Bb Minor - 28 Seconds’,
‘C Major - 39 Seconds’,
‘C# Minor - 14 Seconds’,
‘D Major - 12 Seconds’,
‘Eb Major - 37 Seconds’,
‘F# Minor - 18 Seconds’,
‘G Major - 23 Seconds’

Materials

Beige PLA Afinia 3D prints 12.7 x 12.7 x 3.5cm

Openframeworks C++ program
